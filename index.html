what is cameron's genius?

Over the last year, Cameron has been working on an NFC/RFID reader for his school cafeteria.

At the beginning of the 2023-2024 school year, he questioned why this system wasn't already implemented and that is when this project kicked off.

As he was working on this project in his free time, he noticed that the the sensor he purchased was not high enough quality, so it only worked half the time.

Once the new sensor was purchased it was a lot more consistent, but there were still a ton of wires. He tried to tape these wires in different ways, but it was still really really hard to keep them in place (especially if they are shaken or bumped).

After doing some more research, there was a version that sits on top of the arduino seamlessly, eliminating all of the wires needed.

Once the board was all soldered, he assembled the device in a laser cut box, and began coding both the arduino code, and the code for the app on the computer.

After 2 months of beta testing in the LPC with a group of 25-50 with RFID cards, he was able to have the cards ordered for the upcoming year.

The day before school he worked from 8:30 AM to 4:00 PM encoding every student and faculty card with data.

Now, we have a fully functional RFID system, and he hopes to add apple pay and google wallet cards, to improve ease of access with students.
